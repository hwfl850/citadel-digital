Our Story

Citadel Digital started with a simple observation: most small and mid-sized businesses are great at what they do, but their online presence is managed manually, inconsistently, or not at all.

We founded Citadel on the belief that every business deserves the same digital tools and automation that large companies use to dominate their market. Using our expertise, we build digital systems and keep them running so our clients can focus on the work they do best.
