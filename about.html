About Us

Built by people who've been in your shoes

We started Citadel Digital because we saw how many good businesses were being left behind online. We're here to change that.

Our Story

Citadel Digital started with a simple observation: most small and mid-sized businesses are great at what they do, but they're running their online presence manually, inconsistently, or not at all. We founded the company to close that gap.

We believe every business deserves the same digital tools and automation that large companies use to dominate their market. So we build those systems, great websites and smart automation, and we keep them running, so our clients can focus on the work they do best.
